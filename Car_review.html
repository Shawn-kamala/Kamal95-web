Overview

The Lamborghini Aventador is a mid-engine sports car produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini from 2011 until 2022. Named after a Spanish fighting bull, the Aventador was the successor to the Murciélago.
It was known for its 6.5 L V12 engine, available in several power outputs ranging from 690 hp to 770 hp in the Aventador Ultimae.
The car featured a distinctive design with a carbon-fiber monocoque and was available as a coupé or a roadster. It represented a leap in performance and technology, setting new standards in the super sports car sector

General Specifications
